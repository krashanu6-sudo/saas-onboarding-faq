Product Name: ScaleFlow

Overview:
ScaleFlow is a SaaS platform that helps teams manage projects, automate workflows, and track performance in one place.

Getting Started:
To get started with ScaleFlow, create an account using your email address and verify your email. After login, you can create your first workspace.

Account Setup:
Users can update their profile, change passwords, and manage notification settings from the account settings page.

Integrations:
ScaleFlow supports integrations with GitHub, Slack, and Google Drive. Integrations can be enabled from the integrations section in settings.

API Access:
Developers can access the ScaleFlow API by generating an API key from the developer settings page. The API allows access to projects, tasks, and analytics data.

Troubleshooting:
If you forget your password, use the “Forgot Password” option on the login page to reset it via email.

Billing:
ScaleFlow offers monthly and yearly plans. Billing details and invoices can be managed from the billing section in account settings.

Best Practices:
For best results, organize projects into workspaces and regularly update task statuses to track progress effectively.
